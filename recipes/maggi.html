Maggi

Description

Maggi is one of the most popular instant noodles in India and many other countries. It’s quick to prepare, flavorful, and can be customized with vegetables, eggs, or chicken. The standard preparation takes about 5–7 minutes and makes for a tasty snack or light meal.

Ingredients

  • 1 packet of Maggi Noodles (70g)
  • 1 ¾ cup (about 300 ml) water
  • 1 packet of Maggi Tastemaker Masala (comes inside the packet)

Steps

  1. Boil water
    • Heat 1 ¾ cups of water in a pan.
  2. Add vegetables (optional)
    • If using chopped onions, tomatoes, or carrots, add them into the boiling water and cook for 1–2 minutes.
  3. Add noodles
    • Break the noodle cake into 2–4 pieces and add it to the boiling water.
  4. Add tastemaker masala
    • Sprinkle the Maggi masala (seasoning packet) over the noodles. Mix well.
  5. Cook noodles
    • Let it cook on medium flame for 2–3 minutes, stirring occasionally.
    • If you like soupy Maggi, add a little extra water.
    • For dry Maggi, let the water evaporate fully while stirring.
  6. Serve hot
  7. Transfer to a bowl, garnish with coriander leaves or chili flakes, and enjoy!
