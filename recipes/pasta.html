Pasta

Description

Red Sauce Pasta is a comforting and flavorful dish made with a rich tomato-based sauce, simmered to perfection with garlic, onions, and aromatic herbs. The sauce clings beautifully to tender pasta, creating a hearty and satisfying meal that’s both simple and delicious. With its vibrant red color and bold flavors, this dish is a timeless favorite in many households.

Whether enjoyed on its own with a sprinkle of Parmesan cheese or paired with garlic bread and a fresh salad, Red Sauce Pasta makes for a versatile meal that works perfectly for both busy weeknights and cozy weekend dinners. Its balance of tangy tomatoes, fragrant spices, and a touch of sweetness makes it a crowd-pleaser for kids and adults alike.

Ingredients

  • 200 g (about 2 cups) pasta of your choice (penne, spaghetti, or fusilli)
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 medium onion, finely chopped
  • 3–4 garlic cloves, minced, 2 large tomatoes (blanched and pureed) or 1 cup canned tomato puree
  • 2 tablespoons tomato ketchup (optional, for sweetness)
  • ½ teaspoon red chili flakes (adjust to taste)
  • ½ teaspoon dried oregano. ½ teaspoon dried basil (or use Italian seasoning blend)
  • Salt to taste
  • Freshly ground black pepper, to taste
  • Fresh basil leaves, for garnish

Steps

  1. Cook the Pasta
    • Boil water in a large pot with a pinch of salt and a few drops of oil.
    • Add the pasta and cook according to the package instructions until al dente.
    • Drain and set aside, reserving about ½ cup of pasta water.
  2. Prepare the Sauce Base
  3. Make the Red Sauce
  4. Combine Pasta with Sauce
  5. Serve
